Have you ever clicked a link only to find that the website you were trying to visit is no longer online? The Internet Archive is a useful tool to see what some websites looked like in the past.

This bookmarklet just makes it easy to look up pages on the Internet Archive (no more copying and pasting long URLs). If you come across a webpage you want to look up on the Internet Archive, just activate this bookmarklet to do so.
